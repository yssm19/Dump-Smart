What is Dump Smart?

Dump Smart is a secure, membership-based drop-off site for non-food household junk. Our goal is to provide homeowners and small contractors with a clean, monitored location where they can responsibly dispose of bulky items without relying on random dumpsters or illegal dumping. With 24/7 surveillance and strict rules, we ensure a safe and respectful environment for all users.
